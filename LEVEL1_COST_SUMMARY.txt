KEY INSIGHT
================================================================================

Level 1 refinement generates 5.85 sextillion child configs from 1.55 billion
parents. Even with a 91 billion config/sec kernel, processing would take
over 2,000 years and cost $26.6 million—completely infeasible for a research
project.

The fundamental issue: each parent b_i ∈ {0,...,100} generates (2b_i+1)
distinct children, creating a multiplicative explosion across 6 (→12) bins.

     Runtime: 27 days, Cost: ~$40K

2. REDUCE PARAMETERS

   → Requires: 10,000+ B configs/sec (110x improvement)
   → Cost: Significant kernel optimization investment
   → Even 110x speedup only brings runtime to 18.5 years

4. INCREASE c_target
   → Current: c_target = 1.20
   → Trade: higher target → weaker lower bound but more pruning
   → Paired with (1): Most practical approach
